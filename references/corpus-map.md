# Corpus Map

Use this file when the user asks for "核心观点", "前后变化", "为什么后来变了", or "哪些观点是稳定的".

## Stable Themes

- Consume for reliability, not for abstract pride.
- Use purchasing power and visible standards as a shortcut to reality.
- Treat brands, cities, and credentials as sorting devices.
- Attack self-consoling narratives such as "吃苦有用" or "标准不重要".
- Prefer decisive judgments over balanced discussion.

## Phase Split

### 2023-2024

Common signals:

- More purchasing-power observation and bottom-layer recording.
- More anti-empty-talk and anti-self-deception framing.
- More curiosity about what money buys in different places.
- Still sharp, but less systematized around the later class-label vocabulary.

### 2025

Common signals:

- "Apple vs Android" becomes a generalized social classification tool.
- Sam's Club, Tesla, direct retail, major-city infrastructure, and degree filtering become stronger identity markers.
- Live-stream interaction grows more confrontational and compressed.
- Style becomes easier to parody because the surface phrases repeat more often.

## Distillation Priorities

When compressing his worldview, preserve these layers in order:

1. Decision rule
2. Social signal behind the rule
3. Example object or scene
4. Boundary or contradiction

Example:

- Weak: "He likes Apple and Sam's Club."
- Better: "He treats mainstream premium brands as error-reduction tools, then extends that consumer logic into judgments about standards, class display, and city quality."

## Common Contradictions

- Early concern for lower-layer reality versus later sharper symbolic exclusion.
- Personal non-elite background versus frequent credential sorting.
- Claimed realism versus frequent overextension from anecdote to social law.
- "Speaking truth" framing versus entertainment-driven escalation in live settings.

Call these contradictions out plainly when the user asks for evaluation.
